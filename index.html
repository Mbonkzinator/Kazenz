About Us

Kazenz Labour & Professional Services was founded in 2013 by two black South African man as an organisation aimed at providing a unique solution to satisfy cutomers needs and wants towards First Aid, Occupational Health and Safety and other various services. We are a Health and Wealth Sector Education Training Authority (HWSETA), QCTO accredited and Department of Labour accredited. Our Assessors and Moderators are registered with HWSETA & HPCSA, along with registered paramedics and registered Health and Safety Officers with SAIOSH bodies who can assist Organization and contractors in safety file complilations as well as Health and Safety consultant duties .Kazenz Labour & Professional services' role is to promote the safety and health of all working men and women by setting and enforcing advisory role, providing training, outreach and education, establishing partnerships, and encouraging continual improvement in workplace safety and health. .The company is proudly a Level 1 B-BBEE Contributor

Management

We have a great team comprising of BA Degree holders, holders of London Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Diploma in Marketing. The team holds various certificates in Safety and training, e.g. NQF Level 4 ODETD Practice, Moderator, Facilitation certificate, Coaching and Administration, Assessor certificate in training, SAMTRAC, INTRO to SAMTRAC, ISO 14001: 2015 and OHSAS 18001: 2007 COMSOC 1, HIRA, First Aid Level 3 Instructor and Fire fighting. We also ensure that qualified groups, black or white, people with disabilities have equal opportunities in the workplace

Moderator

We are greatly supported by an integrated quality management system that underpins every aspect of our operations. We as new leaders in our profession strive to deliver honest and reliable services in selected target markets based on ethical principles along with delivering a comprehensive First Aid and SHEQ service solution on a consultancy and operational basis including unlimited services.

Employee

As an employee in Kazenz, our values are Accountability, Customer satisfaction, Innovation, Integrity, Results, Usability, Quality, Zero-harm philosophy, Teamwork and Collaboration. Our vision is to become a recognised and respected top of the list preferred strategic partner offering the most reliable best value added solutions and high quality services that exceed the expectations of our esteemed customers and stakeholders
